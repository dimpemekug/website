## What is Horologe?

Horologe is the app for watch enthusiasts who want to catalog, manage, and admire their collection in an organized and private way. Brand, reference, prices, photos, warranties, and service records — all in one place, stored locally on your device.

## Key Features

- **Complete database** — brand, model, reference, serial number, caliber, year of production, and much more
- **HD gallery** — photos of every watch, accessories, warranty, caseback, and dial details
- **Collection statistics** — total value, brand and movement breakdown, most worn pieces
- **Status & wishlist** — owned, sold, wishlist, or in repair, with automatic gain calculated when you sell
- **Maintenance log** — completed interventions and service costs, with reminders based on the interval you set
- **Wear log** — mark the days you wear a watch with a tap and discover which pieces you reach for the most
- **Accuracy log** — track the daily rate deviation of your movements over time
- **Document attachments** — keep warranty cards, receipts, and certificates alongside each watch
- **App lock** — protect your collection with Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint
- **Export & backup** — PDF to share, CSV for insurance and inventory, ZIP/JSON for complete local backups
- **Privacy first** — database stored locally on your device (or your personal iCloud), no external servers
- **No subscription** — free, with no ads and no in-app purchases

## Platforms

Available on **iOS** and **Android**.
