## What is Horologe?

Horologe is the app for watch enthusiasts who want to keep their collection organized and private. Purchase prices, dates, photos, accessories, warranties, and service records — all in one place, stored locally on your device.

## Key Features

- **Private database** — prices, dates, and photos stay on your device, no external servers
- **HD gallery** — organize watch photos at full quality
- **Complete details** — accessories, warranty certificates, and technical specs for each piece
- **Service tracking** — log maintenance and servicing history over time
- **Market value** — monitor the overall value of your collection
- **References and wishlist** — note the models you own and the ones you want to acquire
- **No subscription** — one-time purchase, full features forever

## Platforms

Available on **iOS** and **Android**.
