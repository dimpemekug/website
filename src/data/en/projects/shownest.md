## What is ShowNest?

ShowNest is your companion for never losing track of what you're watching. It helps you organize, follow, and discover TV shows, all in one clean and fast app.

## Key Features

- **Find something to watch** — browse Trending, Popular, Top Rated, and Airing Today, or search TMDB's full catalog
- **Watchlist** — add any series and keep track of every episode, automatically organized into up next, coming soon, and ended
- **Track your progress** — sort your shows by recently watched, recently added, newest episode, episodes left, and more
- **New-episode alerts** — get notified the day a new episode of your shows airs
- **Statistics** — hours watched, episodes completed, series finished, and favorite genres in a dedicated tab
- **iCloud sync** — your watchlist, progress, and settings sync automatically across iPhone and iPad
- **No subscription** — free, with no ads and no in-app purchases
