Welcome to my digital space. I build mobile apps and websites with attention to detail, privacy, and user experience.

## Who I am

I'm **dimpemekug**, a developer passionate about technology, design, and everything that's built with code. I like creating simple things that work well and respect the people who use them.

## What I do

- **Mobile apps** — iOS and Android, with SwiftUI, Flutter, and Dart.
- **Web development** — modern sites and applications with Astro, TypeScript, and the full JavaScript ecosystem.
- **WordPress themes** — custom editorial solutions, written in plain PHP with no extra frameworks.
- **Privacy by design** — user data stays where it should: on their devices.

## Projects

- **[DomoMeter](/en/projects/domometer/)** — monitors home utility consumption (electricity, gas, water) in a single dashboard, with no account and no server.
- **[Horologe](/en/projects/horologe/)** — a private catalog for watch collectors, with maintenance tracking and market value.
- **[PT39 News](/en/projects/pt39-news/)** — a local news app for Bagnoli Irpino and Irpinia, with no registration and no third-party tracking.
- **[Time Tracker](/en/projects/time-tracker/)** — tracks time across projects and tasks, with PDF reports and local backup.
- **[WP PT39](/en/projects/wp-pt39/)** — an editorial WordPress theme for digital magazines and news sites, written in plain PHP.
